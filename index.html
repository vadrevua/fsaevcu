Our Mission for
FSAE @ VCU

We are currently in the process of designing our second car the "VCU-002"

The Formula SAE @ VCU team went to our first competition at Lincoln, Nebraska where we competed against 70+ other schools. Entering with our VCU-001 car we learned a lot about the competition and how to better build our next car.

For VCU-002 we are planning to do a 2 year approach in which we spend the first year doing major design work as well as some fabrication. The majority of the second year we will be fabricating as well as testing. We will be taking our VCU-002 car to competition during the 2018-2019 school year
